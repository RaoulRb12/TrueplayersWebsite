The Studio

ABOUT US
01 Who we are

Venture-backed, independent, and built for speed.

TruePlayers is a venture-backed, independent developer focused on PC and console simulation titles. We operate with speed and agility, crafting immersive adventures within a workspace we truly value and enjoy.

02 How we work

Small by design, focused on feel.

By remaining small by design, we facilitate quicker decision-making and a collective focus on perfect gameplay feel. Our philosophy balances technical accuracy with the excitement of a truly fun, adventurous simulation environment.

03 Where we are

On the Cornish coast, in Falmouth.

Located on the Cornish coast, our home in the seaside town of Falmouth provides a vibrant, creative backdrop. It is a setting that inherently emphasizes community and a healthy way of living.
