Technical Artist

Sit at the seam between art and engineering. Shape shaders, pipelines and tools that make our simulations feel tactile, readable and unmistakably ours.

About the role

As Technical Artist at True Players, you're the bridge between what the art team wants to make and what the engine can actually do. You'll own shaders, VFX, lighting pipelines and the authoring tools that let us produce work at a high bar without drowning in content debt.

This is a craft role with serious technical depth. You'll write HLSL, build Houdini and Blender workflows, profile frames, and sweat the difference between something that looks right and something that feels right in motion.

What you'll do

  • Author real-time shaders and materials in Unreal (HLSL + material graphs).
  • Build VFX systems — Niagara, custom particles, procedural effects — for combat, flight and environment.
  • Develop and maintain authoring pipelines between DCC tools (Houdini, Blender, Substance) and the engine.
  • Partner with engineers on rendering features, lookdev and performance.
  • Set and hold the visual bar — from first concept to shipped frame.
  • Build editor tools and automations that save the team hours every week.

We'd love to see

  • 3+ years of shipped experience as a technical artist in games, simulation or real-time visuals.
  • Strong portfolio showing shaders, VFX, lookdev or tooling you've owned end-to-end.
  • Confident in HLSL/GLSL and at least one DCC scripting environment (Python/MEL/VEX).
  • Deep Unreal Engine experience (material editor, Niagara, Blueprints, profiling).
  • A clear aesthetic voice and the ability to articulate why something works.

Nice to have

  • Houdini proficiency and experience building procedural content pipelines.
  • Rigging, animation tooling, or character tech background.
  • Graphics-programming adjacency — render features, compute shaders, post-processing.

How we work

Fully remote, UK-based, small and senior. You'll have the space to own your domain, the context to make the right calls, and teammates who'll push you to do your best work. We protect deep-work time and avoid meetings for meetings' sake.

How to apply

Send a CV, your showreel or portfolio, and a short note on a problem you've recently solved that you're proud of, to careers@trueplayers.com. Breakdowns, gifs, node graphs — anything that shows how you think is more than welcome.
