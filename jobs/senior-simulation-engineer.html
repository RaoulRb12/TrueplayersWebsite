Senior Simulation Engineer

Help us build the physics, systems and tools that sit at the core of our simulation pipeline. You'll work close to the metal, shape foundational tech, and ship features that players and researchers can feel.

About the role

As a Senior Simulation Engineer at True Players, you'll own major parts of our simulation codebase — from rigid-body dynamics and sensor modelling through to real-time networking and the tooling that lets the rest of the team build on top. You'll work alongside a small, senior team where engineering decisions move quickly and your fingerprints are everywhere in the final product.

This is a hands-on role. You'll write production code, profile it, break it, and rebuild it. You'll also shape the architecture and mentor as we grow.

What you'll do

  • Design and implement high-performance simulation systems in C++ (Unreal) and/or Rust.
  • Own physics, flight-dynamics and sensor-modelling subsystems end-to-end.
  • Profile and optimise critical paths across CPU and GPU to hit tight real-time budgets.
  • Build internal tools and pipelines that unlock the rest of the team.
  • Collaborate closely with design, art and research on the feel and fidelity of every system.
  • Help shape our engineering culture — code review, architecture, hiring.

We'd love to see

  • 5+ years of production experience shipping real-time simulation, games or engine tech.
  • Deep fluency in modern C++ (and bonus points for Rust, HLSL/GLSL, or Python tooling).
  • A strong grasp of numerical methods, linear algebra and 3D maths.
  • Experience with Unreal Engine, a custom engine, or a comparable real-time stack.
  • A portfolio, shipped title, research paper or repo you can walk us through.
  • Opinions about fidelity, feel and when each one matters more than the other.

Nice to have

  • Background in aerospace, robotics, autonomous systems or scientific computing.
  • Experience with networked multiplayer or distributed simulation.
  • Contributions to open-source simulation, graphics or physics projects.

How we work

We're fully remote with a UK base, and we operate in UK-friendly hours. The team is small and senior by design — you'll have autonomy, context and a direct line to the people making product calls. We value careful, deliberate work over heroics, and we protect focus time fiercely.

How to apply

Send a CV, a short note on why this role and a link to something you've built to careers@trueplayers.com. If you've got a personal site, a GitHub, a reel, or a paper — send that too. We read every application.
