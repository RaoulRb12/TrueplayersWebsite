Our background

ABOUT US
01 Who we are

Independent, venture-backed, and collaborative.

Founded in 2021, TruePlayers is a venture-backed, independent game developer that values speed and agility. We are a collaborative team that prioritizes a positive workspace and quick, collective decision-making to stay focused on our goals.

02 What we make

Simulation titles for PC and consoles.

The studio crafts immersive simulation titles for PC and consoles, balancing technical accuracy with adventurous gameplay. Our philosophy centers on achieving a perfect gameplay feel within a fun and engaging simulation environment.

03 Where we are

Based on the Cornish coast, in Falmouth.

Based in the seaside town of Falmouth on the Cornish coast, the studio operates against a vibrant and creative backdrop. This location emphasizes a healthy way of living and a strong sense of community that inspires our daily work.
